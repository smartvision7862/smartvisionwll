Comprehensive Building Infrastructure Contracting

Smart Vision WLL is a premier accredited **MEP Services Qatar** contractor, delivering mechanical, electrical, and plumbing solutions. Our Grade A certified engineering teams design, install, test, and commission structural MEP systems that conform to the highest safety and regulatory standards set by local Qatari government entities, including **Kahramaa** and the **Qatar Civil Defence Department (QCDD)**.

Our **Mechanical (HVAC)** division delivers high-capacity climate control solutions to counter Qatar's extreme summer weather. We construct chilled water plants, variable refrigerant flow (VRF) multizone systems, air handling units (AHUs), fresh air handling units (FAHUs), and ventilation networks. These systems are calculated for high air change rates and optimized energy efficiency, reducing operational costs while ensuring premium indoor air quality.

Our **Electrical** contracting services include installing low-voltage (LV) switchgears, main distribution boards (MDBs), sub-distribution boards (SMDBs), submain cabling, diesel backup generators, and automatic transfer switches (ATS). We design structural grounding systems, lighting layouts, and KNX-based building automation channels. This ensures safe, steady power distribution across commercial spaces, hotels, malls, and industrial complexes in Doha.

Our **Plumbing** and Public Health department handles commercial booster pump installations, hot/cold water distribution networks, reverse osmosis water filtration units, grease traps (interceptors), and storm/sanitary drainage systems. Every detail is optimized to meet the sanitary codes and structural parameters required for municipal and civil approvals.

Accredited Engineering Scope

  • Kahramaa Compliance: Cable sizing, electrical calculations, panel designs, and grounding inspections certified to pass Kahramaa approvals.
  • QCDD Fire Suppression: Automatic wet/dry sprinkler networks, fire hydrant loops, FM-200 gas suppression, and fire pump rooms.
  • HVAC Energy Balancing: Testing, Adjusting, and Balancing (TAB) operations to audit air volume flow, static pressure, and chiller thermal exchange.
  • Preventative Operations: Annual maintenance contracts (AMC) covering thermal inspections of panels, coil cleanings, and pipe flushes.
