Service FAQs & Client Guidance

What is the difference between Cat6 and Cat6A cabling?

Cat6A is designed to support 10Gbps network speeds over a full 100 meters and features extra shielding to prevent crosstalk, while Cat6 supports up to 1Gbps.

Do you supply certification reports for network cables?

Yes. Every structured cabling project includes a comprehensive Fluke DTX/DSX test report certifying link speed and termination quality.

Which enterprise cybersecurity firewalls do you install?

We primarily install and manage Sophos, Fortinet, and Cisco Meraki firewalls to protect local business networks.
