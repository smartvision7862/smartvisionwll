Service FAQs & Client Guidance

What is the legal video storage requirement for CCTV in Qatar?

Under Qatar's Ministry of Interior Security Systems Department (MOI SSD) regulations, commercial entities must retain continuous video surveillance logs for at least 120 days.

Are Hikvision and Dahua systems fully approved in Qatar?

Yes, select NDAA-compliant and certified systems from Hikvision, Dahua, Axis, and Bosch are fully approved for corporate integration across Doha.

How long does it take to obtain an SSD compliance certificate?

The inspection and SSD certificate issuance process typically takes between 2 to 4 weeks after physical hardware setup and configuration.
